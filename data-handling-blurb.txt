HOW I HANDLE YOUR DATA (plain-English, reusable in sales conversations and on the site)

Your customers' information belongs to you. When a homeowner chats with your
assistant, their name and number go straight to you and nowhere else. I never sell, rent,
or reuse your data or your customers' data. The only services that ever touch it are the ones
needed to run the assistant, like the AI provider that powers the conversation and the secure
tool that delivers the lead to you. You can ask me to delete any data at any time.
